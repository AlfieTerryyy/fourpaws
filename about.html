Get to know me

Get to know me! My name is Lia, and I am the owner of Four Paws Dog Walking. I have been working with dogs since 2019 and hold qualifications in animal management, canine training and behavior, animal-assisted therapy, and canine first aid. Currently, I am pursuing a canine holistic health course, with plans to expand my knowledge through further studies. Although my professional journey with dogs began six years ago, my passion for them started at a very young age. I always knew my purpose was to be around dogs, and this feeling only deepened when I got my German Shepherd cross, Athena, at 19. Throughout my teenage years, I struggled with my mental health, but then Athena provided me with unconditional love, companionship, and the motivation to regain control of my life. She required a lot of attention and training, which strengthened our bond and fueled my passion even further. Now, at a happy and fulfilling stage in my life, I am dedicated to making a positive impact on every dog I meet. I wanted to create a business where the dogs’ needs always come first. Building strong connections with my clients and treating their dogs as if they were my own is incredibly important to me. It’s well known that dogs bring so many positive experiences into human lives—this is my start to returning the favor.
