Four Paws Services and Prices

Group Walks

Socialize your dog in one of our groups!

I take up to 3 dogs from separate houses out for exercise and play! This is a great option for any dog who doesn't get the chance to socialize as often as they’d like.

£16 per group

*Group walks are always an hour session*

Solo Walks

1-1 Attention on our solo walks! Perfect for reactive dogs, highly anxious dogs, oldies, young pups, and any dog who wants that one-on-one time!

£12 for 30 minutes + £8 for an additional dog from the same household

£16 for 1 hour + £12 for an additional dog from the same household

Introductions

FREE introduction session!

This is a 30-minute session, where I meet you and your dog. This is a great opportunity for you to get to know me as your dog walker and for me to learn a little bit about your dog. Once this and our client intake forms are completed, I can walk your dogs!

*Walking available in Theale, Calcot, Tilehurst, Aldermaston, Pangbourne, and the surrounding areas*
